# Landing Page Generation Prompt
# For: Synergetics Hub — Parcel & Delivery Management SaaS

---

## PROMPT TO GIVE THE AI

---

Design a bold, modern, artistic SaaS landing page for a product called **"ParcelFlow"** — 
a multi-tenant parcel and delivery management platform built for logistics companies, 
courier services, and delivery businesses of any size.

---

## BRAND IDENTITY

- **Product name:** ParcelFlow (by Synergetics Hub)
- **Tagline:** *"Every parcel. Every branch. One platform."*
- **Sub-tagline:** *"From intake to doorstep — manage your entire delivery operation in one place."*
- **Tone:** Confident, modern, professional but not corporate-stiff. Think Notion meets Stripe meets a logistics company.
- **Color direction:** Deep navy or charcoal as the base, with a vibrant accent — electric orange, neon green, or electric blue. High contrast. Dark mode aesthetic with light sections for breathing room.
- **Typography feel:** Bold oversized headlines, clean sans-serif body text. Think editorial.
- **Visual style:** Glassmorphism UI cards, subtle grid/dot backgrounds, floating dashboard mockup screenshots, smooth gradient blobs, motion-hinted layouts (even if static).

## DESIGN INSTRUCTIONS FOR THE AI

- Use a **dark base** (#0a0a0f or similar deep navy/charcoal) with **light sections** for contrast
- Accent color: **electric orange (#FF6B2B or similar)** — use for CTAs, highlights, and active states
- Secondary accent: **electric blue or teal** for the Parcel Hub card
- **Glassmorphism** for feature cards — frosted glass effect with subtle borders
- **Bento grid** layout for the features section — varying card sizes
- **3D perspective mockup** in the hero — a floating dashboard screenshot tilted slightly
- **Smooth gradient blobs** in the background of hero and CTA sections
- **Micro-details**: subtle dot grid or line grid backgrounds, thin divider lines, small badge labels
- **Motion hints**: even in a static design, suggest hover states, transitions, and scroll animations with annotations or visual cues
- **Mobile responsive** layout considerations noted in the design
- Font pairing suggestion: **Inter or Geist** for body, **Cal Sans or Syne** for headlines

## WHAT TO GENERATE

Generate a **full-page Figma-ready or HTML/CSS landing page design** with all 12 sections above.
If generating code, use **Tailwind CSS** with a dark theme.
If generating a design description, provide section-by-section layout specs with color codes, spacing, and component details.
The design should feel like it belongs alongside Vercel, Linear, and Stripe in terms of visual quality.
